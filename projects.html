Black Women in STEM

The goal is to build a community of Black Women Engineering and Tech. A reminder that you are not alone. A space to share experiences, learn from tips and uplift.

Are you a black woman in Science Technology Engineering and Maths (STEM)? Have you constantly found yourself in teams/environments where you're an only? This space aims to foster conversations and connections, a constant reminder that you're not alone. While simultaneously learning from inspiring women. It aims to provide both professional and social networking opportunities, hold space and share ideas as well as listen to the most valuable thing you own- your story Whether you were in STEM and left, are looking to get into a STEM field or are currently in the space you're welcome to join. Looking forward to having chats, possible mentorship, networking sessions, socials and a fun time overall. As this is a new group and the initiative is still being setup, you're encouraged to suggest some events you'd like.

You can join our Slack space here to stay updated.
